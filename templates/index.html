Electronic Gadget Addiction Prediction Using Machine Learning

| Project Overview

Machine Learning Modeling & Process

Our system leverages a rich dataset of 12 behavioral and physiological features, including Screen Time, Unlock Frequency, and Heart Rate. We utilize advanced data synthesis to model realistic user patterns across different addiction levels.

We employ a robust Voting Classifier ensemble that combines multiple base models to ensure reliable and consistent predictions. This approach minimizes bias and maximizes detection accuracy for digital addiction risks.

Beyond simple prediction, the platform generates Dynamic Wellness Reports. It provides tailored risks, strategies, and countermeasures based on the individual's digital habits, promoting a healthier lifestyle.

Physiological Sync

Analyzing Heart Rate and stress indicators to provide a deeper understanding of how gadget usage impacts your physical well-being.

Wellness Reports

Transforming complex AI analysis into actionable health reports with specific suggestions and preventive countermeasures.

Voting Classifier

An advanced Ensemble Machine Learning approach ensuring high-confidence predictions by combining the strengths of multiple algorithms.

Usage Analytics

Deep dive into screen time, unlocks, and late-night usage patterns to identify the early warning signs of digital dependency.
